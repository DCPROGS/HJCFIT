Occupancies
===========

The start and end occupancies can be computed one of two way. They can be the equilibrium
occupancies determined from the equation: 

\f\[
  \phi_A = \phi_A {}^e\mathcal{G}_{AF}(s=0) {}^e\mathcal{G}_{FA}(s=0)\\
  \phi_F = {}^e\mathcal{G}_{FA}(s=0) {}^e\mathcal{G}_{AF}(s=0) \phi_F\\
  \phi_A \cdot u_A = 1\\
  \phi_F \cdot u_F = 1
\f\]
